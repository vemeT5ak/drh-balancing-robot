Dual Axis Gyroscope - IDG300

$58.50


The IDG-300 MEMS gyroscope is an integrated dual-axis angular rate sensor designed for consumer and industrial applications requiring low cost, small size and high performance. The IDG-300 gyro leverages InvenSense’s patented gyro, MEMS and wafer-scale integration technologies to deliver unprecedented cost, size, functionality and manufacturing advantages.

The IDG-300 gyro includes all the integrated electronics required for complete application-ready functionality. Factory trimmed scale factors eliminate the need for external active components and end-user calibration. The IDG-300 gyro is lead-free and Green Compliant.

Applications

  • Inertial Measurement Units (IMUs)
  • Handheld GPS Navigation Devices
  • Radio controlled helicopters
  • Toys and game devices
  • Robotic and power tools
  • Antenna positioning
  • Remote control

Features

  • Integrated X- and Y-axis gyro on a single chip
  • Factory set full scale range of ±500°/sec
  • Low offset voltage
  • Integrated low-pass filters
  • Integrated reset switches for high-pass filters
  • Superior vibration rejection over a wide frequency range
  • High cross-axis isolation by design
  • 3V single supply operation
  • 5000 g shock tolerance
  • RoHS Compliant (Completely Lead Free)

Benefits

  • Single chip dual-axis gyro sensor
  • Small footprint
  • Low cost dual-axis gyro solution
  • Integrated electronics for complete application functionality
  • Inherent performance improvements:
  • High temperature & humidity resistance
  • Superior shock & vibration tolerances
  • Low cross-axis sensitivity
  • Compatible with standard SMT processes
