Wiimote 3-Axis Accelerometer module - ADXL330

$24.50

Affection for wiimote, We tried to source the same accelerometer it uses. But I even get a quote of about $138 from our local supplier for an Accelerometer module... No, never for me.

Being angry about their pricing, we managed to fabricate out PCBs and solder out the ADXL330, with 2.54mm pin header which it could insert into Arduino easily.

Caution: plz use 3.3V for its power supply!




Features

  • 3 axis sensing
  • Small, low-profile package
    441.45mm LFCSP
  • Low power 180A at 1.8V (typical)
  • Single-supply operation 1.8 to 3.6V
  • 10,000 g shock survival
  • Excellent temperature stability
  • BW adjustment with a single capacitor per axis
  • RoHS/WEEE lead-free compliant 

Specifications

# of Axes 3
Range +/- 3g
Sensitivity 300 mV/g
Sensitivity Accuracy (%) 10n/a
Output Type Analog
Typical Bandwidth (kHz) 1.6kHz
Noise Density (/s/rtHz) 280
Voltage Supply (V) 1.8 to 3.6
Supply Current (max) 0.18mA
Temp Range (C) -25 to 70C
Package 4mm x 4mm LFCSP
